Accommodations you can do in Maui Hawaii are: 

## 1. Four Seasons Resort Maui at Wailea
4.7 •Hotel

![This a hotel in maui hawaii](/images/hotel1.jpg)

Where: 3900 Wailea Alanui Dr, Kihei, HI 96753.

Why stay here: It’s one of the top-luxury resorts in Maui, and part of the well-known Four Seasons chain. 

Vibe: Upscale, resort-style, great for couples, honeymooners, or anyone who wants premium service and facilities.

Good to know: If you want something more budget-friendly, this may be at the higher end of cost.

## 2. Grand Wailea, A Waldorf Astoria Resort
4.5 •Hotel

![This a hotel in maui hawaii](/images/Resort2.jpg)

Where: 3850 Wailea Alanui Dr, Wailea, HI 96753.

Why stay here: Massive tropical-resort feel, lush grounds, lots of amenities. The “largest private employer on the island” is said to be this resort. 

Vibe: Big resort, lots of activity, good if you like a lively environment with many facilities and things to do.

Good to know: If you prefer quieter boutique stays, this might be more “resort large” than “intimate”.

## 3. Montage Kapalua Bay
4.7 •Resort hotel

![This a hotel in maui hawaii](/images/hotel3.jpg)

Where: 1 Bay Dr, Lahaina, HI 96761.

Why stay here: Villa-style accommodations, which often means more space (good for families or groups) and an upscale feel. 

Vibe: More residential luxury, quieter and perhaps more secluded than big mega-resorts.

Good to know: Villas typically cost more, especially if you want multiple bedrooms.

## 4. Andaz Maui at Wailea Resort
4.6 •Hotel

![This a hotel in maui hawaii](/images/hotel4.jpg)

Where: 3550 Wailea Alanui Dr, Wailea, HI 96753.

Why stay here: A bit more modern / trendy design, stylish amenities, great for design-savvy travellers. 

Vibe: Chic, contemporary, good mix of luxury plus design-forward aesthetic.

Good to know: If you’re after the ‘classic resort’ feel, this might be a bit more modern than that.

## 5. Hotel Wailea
4.8 •Hotel

![This a hotel in maui hawaii](/images/hotel5.jpg)

Where: 555 Kaukahi St, Wailea, HI 96753.

Why stay here: Smaller, more intimate – an adults-only feel (depending on specific room types/policies) in a luxurious setting. 

Vibe: Quiet, boutique, ideal for couples, honeymooners, or anyone wanting a more peaceful stay.

Good to know: May have fewer on-site amenities than the mega-resorts (but that might be exactly what you want).
