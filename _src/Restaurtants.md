Restaurtants you can do in Maui Hawaii are:

## 1. Mama’s Fish House
4.8 •Seafood restaurant

![This a restaurtant in maui hawaii](/images/rest1.jpg)

Location: 799 Poho Pl, Pāʻia, HI 96779.

Why it’s special: This longstanding family-owned seafood destination is often called an icon of Maui dining. It’s set on a beautiful cove and focuses on fresh island-caught fish. 

Vibe: Upscale, scenic, ideal for a special occasion.

Tip: Reservations strongly recommended; arrive with some time to savor the setting.

## 2. Merriman’s Kapalua
4.7 •Restaurant

![This a restaurtant in maui hawaii](/images/rest2.jpg)

Location: 1 Bay Club Pl, Lahaina, HI 96761.

Why it’s special: Known for its farm-to-table philosophy, highlighting locally-grown produce and island-sourced ingredients. 

Vibe: Upscale but comfortable, great for a memorable dinner with local flavor.

Tip: Ask about seasonal dishes — Maui’s produce changes and it affects the menu.

## 3. Tin Roof Maui
4.6 •Hawaiian restaurant

![This a restaurtant in maui hawaii](/images/rest3.jpg)

Location: 360 Papa Pl Ste 116, Kahului, HI 96732.

Why it’s special: A fantastic local-favorite spot offering good food at more moderate prices; a boost if you want something less formal.

“Hands down South Maui Fish company … we visited twice!” — local recommendation for repeat dining. 

Vibe: Casual, lively, local-friendly.

Tip: Perfect for lunch or a relaxed dinner — no need for ultra-formal dress.

## 4. Paia Fish Market Restaurant
4.6 •Seafood restaurant

![This a restaurtant in maui hawaii](/images/rest4.jpg)

Location: 100 Hana Hwy, Pāʻia, HI 96779.

Why it’s special: A top choice for fresh seafood in a relaxed setting; good for lunch or casual dinner.

Vibe: Laid-back, beach-town feel.

Tip: It’s in Pāʻia — handy stop if you’re doing the road to Hana drive or exploring the north shore.

## 5. Monkeypod Kitchen by Merriman – Wailea
4.5 •Hawaiian restaurant

![This a restaurtant in maui hawaii](/images/rest5.jpg)

Location: 10 Wailea Gateway Pl B-201, Kīhei, HI 96753.

Why it’s special: Offers both a fun menu and a lively ambiance — good if you want dessert + drinks + good food.

Vibe: Trendy but not overly formal — works well for groups or casual upscale.

Tip: Check their happy hour specials if you’re looking to enjoy something a bit more budget-friendly.
